Albion ruin Beavers 100% record

West Middlesex Sunday Football League
Premier Division

Chiswick Albion 3 Beavers 2

                                   
                                                  Liam White (2)                  Sean McGee
                                                                    Peter Hughes                  Simon Lamb


Chiswick Albion twice came from behind to end Beavers 100% league record in this deserved win. With the three White brothers in a starting line up for the first time, they all made contributions towards the win. Liam scored a brace; Ben drove on the midfield and 15 year's old Adam held the ball up well and created chances. Things started badly though for the Albion as Richard Griffin crossed the ball into his own area and Sean McGee scored this after only five minutes. Albion's young side was not fazed and Richard Ralph and Phil
Heggie both went close. The equaliser came when Liam White rounded the keeper to fire home. Albion's new number one Peter Sousa twice pulled off fine saves to deny Beavers but was beaten when Simon Lamb beat the off side trap to take the lead. Again Albion came back and Peter Hughes
converted Dave Gaffney's corner with a bullet header. The victory was
secured when Liam White picked his spot from the edge of the area to give Reilly no chance. Another plus for the Chiswick based side was Paul Harris who made an impressive debut at the heart of the Albion defence.
