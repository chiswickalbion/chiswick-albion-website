Seven from seven as Albion win 10-1 at Meadhurst

Meadhurst Sunday 1 Chiswick Albion 10

                                                                                   
                                                                                      Dean Francis
                                                                                                                  Adam White (2)
                                                                                                                  Liam White (2)
                                                                                                                  Robert Dawson (3)
                                                                                                                  Vojin Soskic
                                                                                                                  Danny Rouco

Chiswick Albion made it seven wins from seven played in the West Middlesex Premier
Division to remain top of the table after defeating mid-table Meadhurst Sunday 10-1.
The Albion started well and a surging run down the left flank by Adam White saw his
cross-converted at the far post by Dean Francis. Adam White had a couple of chances he spurned, until with the help of Vojin Soskic he made it 2-0. The Albion then created a
wonderful goal from their own goal-line, and with most of the team involved, transferred the ball to the other end of the pitch where Liam White turned it in.
The second half was a lot easier as Chiswick scored at will. First Robert Dawson was on hand to turn in Dani Rouco's cross. Meadhurst then picked up on a defensive mistake to take their only real chance of the match.  This spurred on Chiswick and Liam White put away Rouco's cross. Another cross this time by Dawson found young striker Adam White alone at the far post to head in. Dawson then completed his hat trick with two quick goals, one with his right foot, as Meadhurst fell apart. Defender Vojin Soskic ran half the length of the pitch to convert number nine, and Dani Rouco completed the rout with a simple header.
A two-week League winter break leaves the Albion closing in on four fronts, and a big 2003 is anticipated.

Manager's Man of the Match - Robert Dawson
