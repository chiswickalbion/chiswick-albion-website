A Liam White hat trick is enough in the Open Cup

  West Middlesex Sunday Football League
Ace Awards Open Cup Round 2

Chiswick Albion 5 Laleham Park 1


                                                                              Liam White (3)
                                                                              Robert Dawson
                                                                              Anthony Clarke

Chiswick Albion progressed to the quarterfinals of the Ace Awards Open Cup after finally overcoming a spirited Laleham Park. Park play two divisions below the Albion, but paid little respect for the highflying Albion. After a couple of Albion half chances, Laleham against the run of play following a long boot from keeper Pusey took a shock lead. Both sides were
finding the going tough on the muddy surface, but the level of skill was high, especially for
Albion's Dean Francis who glided all over the pitch. Liam White at this point woke up and ran strongly at the Park defence, and played a one two with his younger brother Adam and powered the returned ball home. Then again Adam White put in a good cross, which Liam slid in from close in for an interval lead. The Albion were stronger side in the second period and Anthony Clarke crossed, Robert Dawson controlled in an instant before smashing in an unstoppable shot.
Pusey then felled Adam White in the box, and up stepped Liam White to complete his hat trick, and ninetieth in total for the season. Francis in the middle of Albion's best moves then crossed and Clarke made no mistake. A good solid team performance, and the Albion now play Venceremos in the next round.

Match Report Mark White
