That man Velinor saves the Albion's blushes, and as they remain top

  West Middlesex Sunday Football League Premier Division

The Rising Sun 3 Chiswick Albion 4

                                                                                           
                                                                                                  Dean Francis
                                                                                                                                    Liam White
                                                                                                                                    Alan Sime
                                                                                                                                    Karl Velinor

Chiswick Albion remain top of the West Middlesex Premier Division table, but they needed a late winner from Karl Velinor to see off a spirited The Rising Sun. Albion started slowly and it was the Sun who pressed early on. Somewhat against the run of play Vojin Soskic crossed and Dean Francis playing up front in place of the injured Adam White slammed home from close in. Then a threaded pass from Anthony Clarke picked out Liam White and the prolific goalscorer scored his 16th of the season. The Albion were now in command and Robert Dawson from fully 40 yards hit a sweet free-kick that was just kept out by Sun's keeper, who also repelled White's
follow up shot. Just before half time the Sun pulled a goal back when Danny Allen who had been a constant threat all game, beat the linesman and Albion defence to reduce the arrears.
The Albion got off to the perfect start in the second period when Francis crossed Alan Sime shot; hit the keeper out to White who hit the post, and back to Sime who this time converted for 3-1. Just as the Albion looked to press on for more goals themselves, a mistake in the defence
allowed the lively Sun's forwards to earn themselves a penalty that was duly converted. The Sun were now on top and had a wicked shot, which hit the crossbar only to bounce clear. An uneasy Albion defence then conceded again with a well-taken goal and it was back to 3-3. Time was ripe to bring on the 18-year-old Karl Velinor as the side was reshuffled. White earned himself a free kick on the edge of the box, and struck it well enough, only for the keeper to fumble it, and up popped that man Velinor with the faintest of touches to score the winner for the second week on the trot.
The Rising Sun gave this young Albion outfit a difficult match, but in the end the Albion were just about worthy winners.

Referee The Rising Sun
