The Albion defeat up and coming Prolifics

Chiswick Albion 4 Chiswick Prolifics 2

                                             
                                          Adam White (2)
                                                      Rob Ursell
                                                    Ben White


Chiswick Albion just shaded the other side from Chiswick; the Prolifics with better finishing from their young strike force. Adam White showed his intent with a shot from 25 yards that came back off the crossbar, but Vojin Soskic headed the
rebound wide. The Albion opened the scoring when Richard Ralph fired in a cross from a corner, and Adam White shot in at the far post. Liam White went close on a couple of occasions, but the Prolifics carved out chances for themselves that should have been converted.
The Albion showed them the way to finish when Soskic hit a shot well that was parried by Collins, but the ball fell to Adam White who crashed the ball home at the far post once again. The Prolifics pulled a goal back from a corner as Mark Keadell powered home a header. The Albion again broke through Ralph who crossed for Rob Ursell to tease the defence before firing home.
From another corner the Prolifics again pulled a goal back to set up an exciting finish. The game was over when Ben White the eldest of the three brothers picked up brother Adam's pass to stroke a left foot shot past Collins in the
Prolifics goal. The Prolifics will do well in the Intermediate Division, and the
Albion is looking good for a crack at the Premier title. Off the Pitch, the White brothers elder sister Sophie could be heard singing "4-2 in your Cup Final"
